Tips for Better Recognition

💡

Good Lighting

Ensure your hand is well-lit from the front. Avoid strong backlighting as it creates silhouettes that reduce landmark accuracy.

📐

Camera Distance

Keep your hand 30–60 cm from the camera. Your full hand and wrist should be clearly visible within the camera frame.

🖐️

Clear Background

A plain, contrasting background helps MediaPipe detect hand boundaries and track the 21 landmarks more reliably.

⏱️

Hold Steady

Hold each gesture completely still for the configured duration (default 1.2 seconds) to register it into your sentence.

⌨️

Keyboard Fallback

No camera? Use the Keyboard Fallback panel on the main screen to type letters directly into the sentence builder.

🔊

Sound Feedback

Enable sound feedback in Gesture Settings to hear a soft click whenever a letter is successfully committed to the sentence.
